PREMIER Nursing COLLEGE is one of the leading Nursing Institutions in the state of Karnataka offering 4 year Degree programme in NURSING running successfully from 15 years established on 2003 in a beautiful city gauribidanur and nice weather and very peaceful place to study ,just 1 hr journey from Bangalore .

The irresistible urge to groom Nursing & endow competent Nurses to serve globally has transformed the plateau into a sanctuary of education.
